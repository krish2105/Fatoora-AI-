# Limitations & Disclaimers

Fatoora AI is designed as a robust SaaS demonstration of UAE e-invoicing readiness. However, there are intentional boundaries and limitations to be aware of:

## 1. Not an Accredited Service Provider (ASP)
**Disclaimer**: Fatoora AI is *ASP-integration-ready*, but this software **does not directly connect to the UAE Federal Tax Authority (FTA)**. Under the 2026 e-invoicing mandate, businesses must route invoices through an ASP. Fatoora AI generates the required structured data format, but you must pair it with an official ASP for legal clearance.

## 2. VAT Estimations
**Disclaimer**: All VAT summaries and reports generated by the platform are **VAT estimates only**. They are not official tax filings. You must review the exported data with a certified accountant before filing your returns on the EmaraTax portal.

## 3. Storage Provider
In local development, the storage provider falls back to a Mock implementation. Files are not physically persisted across sessions unless you provide an S3 or UploadThing key.

## 4. AI Provider Confidence
The AI OCR extraction uses a mock implementation by default. If connected to OpenAI, it may hallucinate data on highly unstructured receipts. A manual user review is *always* required before data is committed to the general ledger.
